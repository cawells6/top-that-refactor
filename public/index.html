THE OFFICIAL RULES

The Goal
  • The first player to play all of their cards wins the game!

Setup
  • Deal 9 cards to each player:
    • 3 Face-Down Cards: Place in a row. (No looking!)
    • 3 Face-Up Cards: Place on top of the Face-Down Cards.
    • 3 Hand Cards: Deal into the player's hand.
  • Start the Game:
    • Place the remaining deck in the center to create the Draw Pile.
    • Flip the top card to start the Play Pile.

How to Play Your Turn
  • Step A: Try to Play a Card
    • You must play a card from your hand that is strictly higher in value than the top card on the Play Pile.
    • No Ties: You cannot play a card of equal value (e.g., a 6 cannot go on a 6).
    • Multiples: You may play multiple copies of the same rank (e.g., three Jacks), if they beat the top card.
    • Refill: If the Draw Pile has cards, you must keep at least 3 cards in your hand. Draw any time you drop below 3.
  • Step B: Can't Play? (The "Stuck" Rule)
    • Pick Up: You must pick up the entire Play Pile and add it to your hand.
    • End Turn: Your turn is over. The next player goes.
    • Backtracking: If you pick up while playing Face-Up or Face-Down Cards, you must clear your hand again before returning to the table cards.

Special Cards (Power Plays)

These cards break the standard rules and can be played on any card.

  • 2 (Reset):
    • Resets the Play Pile value to 2. It is the lowest card, making it easy for the next player to beat.
  • 5 (Copycat):
    • Copies the previous card on top of the pile. If it copies a 2 or 10, it triggers that effect.
    • If a 5 is the starter card (no card beneath), it plays as a normal 5.
  • 10 (Burn):
    • Burns the Play Pile! The pile is completely removed from the game.
    • Play passes to the next person.
    • If a 10 is the starter card (no card beneath), it plays like a normal 10.
  • 4-of-a-Kind:
    • If you play four of the same rank at once (e.g., four 6's), it burns the Play Pile like a 10.
    • Only counts when played in the same turn.
    • The next player takes their turn (no extra turn).

Card Progression

You must clear your cards in this specific order. You cannot skip ahead!

  • Hand Cards:
    • Play these until the Draw Pile is gone and your hand is empty.
  • Face-Up Cards:
    • Unlocked only when your hand is empty. Play one face-up card at a time. If you are unable to play; add the card and the discard pile to your hand.
  • Face-Down Cards:
    • Unlocked only when your Face-Up Cards are gone and your hand is empty. Play one face-down card at a time. You play these cards blind. If you are unable to play; add the card and the discard pile to your hand.
